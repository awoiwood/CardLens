Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Introduction

Card Lens ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Card Lens processes all data locally on your device. Specifically:

  • Camera images are processed on-device using machine learning
  • No images are stored, transmitted, or shared
  • No personal data is collected or transmitted to external servers
  • No usage analytics or tracking data is collected

Camera Access

Card Lens requires camera access to detect playing cards. Camera access is used solely for this purpose:

  • Images are processed in real-time on your device
  • No images are saved to your device's photo library
  • No images are transmitted over the network
  • Camera access can be revoked at any time in your device settings

Data Storage

All data, including app preferences and settings, is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this information.

Third-Party Services

Card Lens does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Card Lens is intended for users 18 years of age or older. Users under this age may not use this app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were made.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store listing.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to delete or modify. You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all local data.
